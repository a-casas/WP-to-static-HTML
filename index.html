japp&go Travel Planner

japp&go Travel Planner is a MERN Stack responsive Web App Project (Backend + Frontend)

What does this app do

  • Assists to organize activities on a trip to Japan.
  • Helps to find amazing and interesting places to visit and add them to your plans list.
  • Provides a map with your chosen POIs. The map updates in real-time when you manage these locations.

The Purpose

«The once passionate inventor was’ completely alone. Lost, he was no longer even able to remember what’ was his motivation for inventing all those gadgets. Now he wanders in the’ dark, in an attempt to remember what his purpose is…»

The Purpose is my entry in the Global Game Jam 2021. Designed & developed in 2 days, hand in hand, with my friend and colleague Mark Sanjuán Solé.

We included the diversifier “The Feels” (sponsored by SONY Interactive Entertainment) that promoted the construction of an emotional story, so I hope you enjoy the story as much as we enjoy developing it.

Scape theDev.dream

«You are a web developer who has been working on an exciting project for several days. Eventually fatigue overcomes you and you fall asleep in front of the laptop.»

My first project at Ironhack, a web-browser based graphic adventure game. It’s a 2D HTML canvas but looks and feels like 3D  (with a fixed view).

Use the W,A,S,D keys to move around. Explore the stage with the mouse. Try to find a way to wake up or get trapped in the dream… forever!
